What is my goal in this game? +
Survive longer than your personal best to gather resources (XP/Gold) that permanently upgrade your future runs, while your NFTs personalize and enhance the experience.
What resets after each session? +
Your character's in-session Level and all randomly selected elemental skills reset to zero. This ensures that every run is a unique challenge.
How do my NFTs affect gameplay? +
NFTs personalize your character and may unlock unique progression tracks or in-game boosts.
What is the Points system? +
Points are a critical on-platform reward for engagement. Earn them by verifying accounts, inviting others, and completing daily tasks.
Is the game free to play? +
Yes. Insomnus is free-to-play. Connect your wallet and begin immediately. No purchase required to enter the dungeon.
What is permadeath? +
Every death ends the current run completely. There are no continues. Your growth and orbs carry forward, but the run is lost forever.
How do orbs work? +
Monsters drop orbs on death. Collect them by running over them. XP orbs level you up. Health, Armor, and Stamina orbs help you survive longer.
Can I play on mobile? +
Yes. Insomnus is fully playable in the browser on both PC and mobile. The controls adapt automatically to touch.
